Before accessing the main menu, let's explain how the program works: 

Program division:
The Orbity program is divided into two main parts: Calculations and Graph Generation.
Follow the recommended steps to use the program efficiently:

Recommendations:

[01] Read the important notices to understand the program's limitations and recommendations;   
[02] Access the main menu to choose the desired service, whether it is a specific calculation or the generation of a graph; 
[03] Enter the number corresponding to the desired service, and follow the instructions to provide the data necessary for the calculation or graph;
[04] Review the results obtained, and if necessary, make adjustments to the input data to obtain more accurate results;    
[05] At the end, you will receive a message asking if you want to return to the main menu or end the program, choose the desired option to continue or end your experience.    
[06] We hope it will be a useful tool for your studies and projects related to educational rocket launching!
